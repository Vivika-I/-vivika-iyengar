Project 1: Landing Page, Blog, Portfolio

This project was created through a combination of HTML and CSS.The first page(landing page) was the first step I took towards using HTML and CSS simultaneously.

The landing page and color choices


The landing page was inspired by my love for colorful abstract paintings.After I saw a particular one with the colors I wanted I went to Adobe colors and found a color scheme that flowed with the painting. Out of the 5 colors i chose to mainly use the Navy blue, the pink and the light blue.I used the navy blue for my background and used the lighter colors to add a little bit of life to the website.Firstly,I used a jumbotron becuase the size of it makes it the first thing you see.This is important because you want your brand/name to be the first thing people see when they open your website. Additionally, the use of containers really helped organize the structure of the website.





The navy blue, the pink and the light blue automatically stuck out to me and I decided to make those my three colors.I chose not to use all 5 because i didnt want it to get too complicated.
